Effective Date: December 24, 2025

At SubtitleTK (accessible via https://www.subtitletk.app), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered subtitle extraction and translation services.

## 1. Information We Collect

**Account Information**: Email address and basic profile details to manage credit balances and history.

**Usage Data**: YouTube/TikTok URLs submitted for processing.

**Payment Data**: Financial transactions are handled by Creem. We do not store credit card details on our servers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

**Service Delivery**: Processing extractions, AI translations, and video downloads.

**Credit Management**: Deducting credits per task and facilitating automated refunds for failed attempts.

**Support**: Troubleshooting video links or account issues.

## 3. Data Retention and Security

**Storage**: We store processed metadata and SRT files in secure cloud storage via unique identifiers (UUIDs).

**Security**: We use industry-standard encryption to ensure your content remains private.

## 4. Third-Party Services

**AI Engines**: We use advanced AI for translation; no PII (Personally Identifiable Information) is sent to these models.

**Payments**: Secured by Creem.

**No Data Selling**: We do not sell or trade your personal data to third parties.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your account and task history at any time.

## 6. Contact Us

For any data-related inquiries, please contact: support@subtitletk.app.
